What We Do

Every Saturday and Sunday, we conduct classes inside the premise of college and routinely visit them for the next five days lest their performance – both academic and non-academic – should go haywire. To make aware, informed and responsible citizens, we help them know about the spectrum of good or bad things happening around through light and comic cartoons. Sports activities are regularly conducted to inculcate the sense of being physically fit necessary for mental growth. We have with us medical camps and sanitation programs to keep a check on illness and create awareness about the cleanliness and hygiene Kids are special, no wonder where they live – in slums or in the palace. They are buzzing with new ideas, waiting to take wing. They need exposure to know their inner selves, their natural flair. So, we arrange for space in the college’s cultural and technical fest where they participate in science exhibitions and cultural programs. Drawing and writing competitions are organized to help them whet their creativity while cashing in on its natural reserve.

Our Story

Bihari more education project (BMEP)-a volunteer-driven initiative by NIT Durgapur students- nurtures and empowers underprivileged children. Our mission is to help kill social illness through the soft and potent dose of education. So, regular teaching and consistent mentoring have been the top priority of BMEP. The journey which started from Bihari more has been well extended to the Ranchi colony and altogether we have impacted above than 120 students along with 100 parents. BMEP is registered as an NGO Bihari More Education and Welfare Society under the West Bengal Societies Registration Act, Reg. No.: S/2L/34434.

What We Pay For

Your good chunk of money is being used to pay school fees. Besides, we have expenses at Medical Camp, Sports Day Event, Sanitation Program, Sessional Events, Children’s Day Celebration, Teacher’s Day Celebration, Educational Trips, etc. In the era of digital India, everybody needs to learn things in a smart way. And to accomplished it, which we thought to establish a smart classroom consisting of the projector and teaching materials for activity-based learning to improve understanding of the Kids. At the same time, we will establish a library for our kids which will have all the necessary books and learning materials.
